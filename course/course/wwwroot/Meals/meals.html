HealthyNotts by Sebastian Hofton

Welcome to the meals page on this page you will find helpful information on what to eat to be healthier and how to make these meals. Diet is a key factor to being healthy thats why we are helping you out by giving you the recipes to use yourself so that you can make healthy food easily.

recipe Chicken and beans:

  1. 3kg of chicken and any amount of kg for green beans above .5 Kg.
  2. heat pan up for 5 minutes then add cooking oil.
  3. place the chicken in pan for 10 minutes then gradually add beans
  4. fry for 15 more minutes then turn the heat off.

Recipe for salmon and potato:

  1. Wash potato and cut into ball shapes
  2. Prepare salmon by adding seasoing until top of salmon is covered up.
  3. Put salmon in oven and cook for 15 minutes
  4. Add potatoes into a dish with oil and place in oven for 10 minutes.

Recipe for Chicken Salad:

  1. 2Kg of chicken cut into small slices
  2. A packet of salad that you should wash then dry
  3. Place cut chicken pieces in the oven for 15-20 minutes unil cooked
  4. Place chicken in the salad and mix in so that the chicken is spread out around the meal.
